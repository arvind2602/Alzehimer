    About

    Welcome to our Alzheimer's Detection App! We are a team of healthcare professionals, software engineers, and researchers who are passionate about improving the lives of people affected by Alzheimer's disease.

    Our app is designed to provide an accurate and accessible means of detecting the early signs of Alzheimer's disease.

    We understand the importance of early detection and intervention in managing this condition, and our app aims to facilitate that process for users.

    • Our team comprises of experts from various fields, including neurology, psychology, and artificial intelligence.
    • We have leveraged our diverse backgrounds and expertise to create an app that is comprehensive, user-friendly, and reliable
    • Our app uses cutting-edge technology, including machine learning algorithms, to analyze user input and provide an accurate assessment of their risk of developing Alzheimer's disease.
